World Coffee House

Since 1934

Established in the spring of 1934, World Coffee House is the world's largest coffee producing corporation. It was founded by Sir Craig McArthur and is headquartered in Milan, Italy.

One fine afternoon, after several months of experimenting and observing, he developed the finest ratio of coffee and milk, along with highly efficient planting and brewing techniques. He couldn't control the joy of hitting such a feat and decided to spread the joy of sipping the same splendid coffee to everyone across the world.

And World Coffee House was born, with a history so rich that you decided to read about it right now.
